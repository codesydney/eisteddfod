Become a Volunteer!

The City of Parramatta Eisteddfod Society Inc. desperately needs NEW VOLUNTEERS to ensure the continuation of the Speech and Drama Eisteddfod in 2022.

Giving your help for just a morning, an afternoon or an evening really does make a difference and allows the Eisteddfod to keep going. Sharing the load means no one is overburdened. Parramatta Eisteddfod gives young people a wonderful opportunity to build their confidence and display their skills in in poetry recital, public speaking and drama in its many forms.

If you can spare approximately 3 1/2 to 4 hrs hours of your time on any of the Saturdays and Sundays in May and June (NOT Mothers' Day or the June long week-end) to sell entrance tickets and programmes, stage manage, or scribe with the Adjudicator, that would be greatly appreciated. You will be clearly instructed in what to do so you feel comfortable in whatever role you choose.

You will need to be at least 16 years of age.

For further details please email Mrs Elaine Hodda OR Phone/SMS Elaine with your availability.

Established in 1951, the City of Parramatta Eisteddfod Society Inc. is a not-for-profit organisation and an association of volunteers whose principal aim is to provide a safe and encouraging performance arts event where the young people of Greater Western Sydney and beyond can showcase their talents. Participants in the Eisteddfod are welcome from any location.
