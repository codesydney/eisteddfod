Privacy Statement

The City of Parramatta Eisteddfod Society Inc has created this privacy statement in order to demonstrate our firm commitment to privacy and adherence to The Australian Privacy Act 1988. The following discloses our information gathering and dissemination practices for this website: www.eisteddfodparramatta.org.au.

Entry and payment details

The City of Parramatta Eisteddfod Society Inc does not collect or use personal information from competitors for any purpose other than for organising and running eisteddfods.
Entry details and are to be lodged via the online eisteddfod competition entry system provided by Stardom, to which there is a link on this website. Before entering personal details, it is important for users to read the Terms of Use and Privacy Statement on the Stardom website. On the Stardom home page, scroll down to the bottom right hand corner and click on "Terms of Use" and "Privacy Policy".
The facility for payment by credit card or debit card of entry fees, etc is provided via the Stardom Online Merchant account established directly with the National Australia Bank (NAB Transact). No credit card or debit card details are retained by the City of Parramatta Eisteddfod Society Inc.

Log Files

We do not use IP addresses to analyse trends and administer the site, we do not track users' movements or gather broad demographic information.

Security

All of our users' information is restricted to the City of Parramatta Eisteddfod Society Inc and Stardom.

Contacting the Webmaster

If you have any questions about the privacy statement, the practices of this site, or your dealings with this Web Site, you can contact Sue Diserens.
