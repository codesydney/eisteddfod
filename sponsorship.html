Sponsorship.

Be associated with the largest performing arts festival in Western Sydney.

Planning is under way for The 70th Annual City of Parramatta Eisteddfod be held in the year 2022 from May to June. The City of Parramatta Eisteddfod Society (Inc) is a not for profit organisation that is seeking sponsorship for the various sections.

Sponsorship of the various Eisteddfod Sections for Speech and Drama(including Public Speaking) is vital to its overall success. Junior competitors are awarded trophies and ribbons/medals and for some sections, cash prizes. Cash prizes are awarded to senior competitors.

The City of Parramatta Eisteddfod is one of the largest Speech & Drama festivals in New South Wales, enabling 2,000 performances from children, teenagers and adults. Be part of this colourful and prestigious event by becoming a sponsor and at the same time lift the "Good Corporate Citizen" status of your business, company, organisation.

Competitors are drawn from all over the Sydney metropolitan area and beyond, so your business, company or service organisation will enjoy widespread exposure.

Sponsorship levels start at $50 which will be allocated towards the awards for one section. Other levels of sponsorship appear below.

$50 to $150 In the Program, your name, business name, company or service organisation will appear on the Sponsor Honour Roll plus immediately under the allocated section heading/s.
$300 In the Program, acknowledged as a "Silver Sponsor". Your name or the name of your Business, Company or Service Organisation will appear throughout the Program under the allocated section headings.
$500 In the Program, acknowledged as a "Gold Sponsor". If applicable, a half page advertisement for your Busines, Company or Service Organisation can be displayed in the program plus your logo will appear on our Website as a Gold Sponsor. This is in addition to the other benefits listed above. You or a representative of your Business, Company or Service Organisation will be invited to attend the performances to present the Awards.
$750 In the Program, acknowledged as a "Gold Sponsor". Benefits as above PLUS a link from our website to your website.
$1000 In the Program, acknowledged as a "Platinum Sponsor". Benefits as above plus, if applicable, special consideration regarding advertising and promotion of your Business, Company or Service Organisation at the Eisteddfod as mutually agreed.
